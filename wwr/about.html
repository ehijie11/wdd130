History

Early 2000s: Zarahemla Rafting Company was founded by a group of friends who shared a passion for whitewater adventure. Starting with a few rafts and a prime location near River Explorer National Park, they introduced people to the thrill of river rafting. Mid-2000s: Their reputation grew quickly as they expanded their fleet and hired experienced guides, focusing on safety and enjoyment. They also embraced environmental responsibility, partnering with local conservation groups. 2010s: Adventure Rapids became a top choice for river adventures, offering various rafting experiences. They adapted during the COVID-19 pandemic by implementing safety measures and continued to provide memorable trips. Present Day: Today, Zarahemla is a respected name in rafting. They offer team-building adventures, educational programs, and environmental initiatives, staying true to their mission of sharing the joy of rafting and preserving nature.

Audience

Our potential customers are looking for exhilaration, excitement, and a sense of accomplishment. They want to experience the thrill of navigating challenging river rapids while enjoying the beauty of nature. Our target audience should be in reasonably good physical condition to participate in white water rafting, as it requires moderate strength and agility. They should be comfortable with physical exertion and outdoor challenges. The ideal age group is between 18 and 45 years old. They are adventurous at heart, with a keen interest in outdoor activities, adrenaline rushes, and exploring natural landscapes.
