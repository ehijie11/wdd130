Melangit River Klungkung Location

Melangit River Bali is located in Bakas Village, Banjarangkan district, in Klungkung Regency. The river is in the western part of Klungkung Regency and the eastern part of Gianyar Regency.

  • From Ngurah Rai International, it will take 1 hour and 25 minutes to drive through Jalan Professor Ida Bagus Mantra, about 44 kilometers.
  • If you are heading from the central Ubud area, such as Monkey Forest Ubud, it will take 50 minutes to drive, a distance of around 21 kilometers.

How To Get There

In Bali, when you book a Bali rafting tour, there will be an offer that the rafting tour price has to include pick up and return transfer. Therefore, if you reserve pickup and return transfer with your Bali river rafting, heading to the river location from your hotel will be easy.

Furthermore, the pickup and return transfer is sharing transportation, which means there will be other guests you don’t recognize in the same car.

However, some travelers would like to visit other places in Bali after rafting tours, such as Bali Safari marine park or Bali bird park. So you need private transport.

Getting private transportation in Bali is easy; you only need to find Bali car rental services with a driver or rent a scooter.

River Length & Rapid Class

While the Melangit River length for rafting trips is around 7,5 kilometers, and it will take a 1.5-hour rafting tour from start point to finish point. Also, Melangit River whitewater rafting in Bali is classified as grade III-IV whitewater rapids class.

So what makes Melangit rafting classified as III-IV rapids class? Even though the river length is not quite long, there are 37 drops on the river. The highest drop on the river is around 3 meters.

Things You Will See On The Melangit River

Furthermore, the Melangit River offers rainforest scenery with a twist and turns on every part of the river, providing a sharp bend that will pump your adrenaline. Therefore, those who love adventure will love the Melangit River. Also, a steep gradient on a particular part of the river creates one to four meters of river waterfalls.

Since the Melangit River, the rafting trip is the most challenging stream for white water rafting in Bali. The river is less polluted, and the green scenery is still well-preserved. Also, the air is always clean because the location is far from the city.

So if you are searching for more adventures and challenging rivers and want fresh air, less polluted water, and a well-preserved green environment, you should join the river rafting Bali on this river.
